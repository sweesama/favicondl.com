Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Mzu favicondl ("we", "our", or "the Service") is a free online tool for downloading and extracting website favicons. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information:

  • Domain names you enter for favicon extraction (processed in real-time, not stored)
  • Language preference (stored locally in your browser via localStorage)
  • Standard web analytics data (page views, browser type, country) if analytics is enabled

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide the favicon extraction and download service
  • To remember your language preference across visits
  • To understand usage patterns and improve the Service

4. Data Storage

All image processing (resizing, format conversion) happens entirely in your browser. No images are uploaded to or stored on our servers. The Cloudflare Worker proxy processes requests in real-time without logging.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to save your language preference. We do not use tracking cookies. Third-party services (analytics, ads) may use their own cookies — please refer to their respective privacy policies.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google Favicon Service — used as a fallback source for favicon images
  • Cloudflare Workers — used for favicon discovery and CORS proxying
  • Google Analytics — used for anonymous usage statistics (if enabled)
  • CDN services (jsDelivr, cdnjs) — used to load open-source libraries

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
