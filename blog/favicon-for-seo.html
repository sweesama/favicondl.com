Why Favicons Matter for SEO (Google Search Results)

In 2019, Google started showing favicons in mobile search results. By 2023, favicons appeared on desktop too. Today, your favicon is a visible part of every Google search listing. Here's why that matters — and how to get it right.

How Favicons Appear in Google Search

In Google search results, your favicon appears:

  • Mobile: Next to your site name and URL, above the title link
  • Desktop: Next to the breadcrumb/URL below the title

If you don't have a favicon (or it doesn't meet Google's requirements), Google shows a generic gray globe icon instead. This immediately makes your listing look less trustworthy compared to competitors with branded favicons.

The CTR Impact

Click-through rate (CTR) is one of the most important SEO metrics. It measures how often people click your listing after seeing it in search results.

A study by Hostiserver found that websites with a favicon saw CTR improvements of up to 20% compared to those without. Why?

  • Visual distinction — Your listing stands out from the wall of text
  • Brand recognition — Returning users recognize your icon before reading the title
  • Perceived professionalism — A branded icon signals a legitimate, maintained website
  • Trust signal — The generic globe icon is often associated with spam or abandoned sites

Google's Technical Requirements

Google has specific requirements for favicons to appear in search results:

RequirementDetails
SizeMultiple of 48px (e.g., 48×48, 96×96, 144×144). Google resizes to 16×16 for display.
ShapeMust be square. Non-square images may be rejected.
URLMust be crawlable — not blocked by robots.txt.
StabilityURL should not change frequently.
ContentMust represent the website's brand. No offensive, inappropriate, or generic content.
DomainMust be served from the same domain as the website (or a subdomain).

Common Reasons Google Doesn't Show Your Favicon

  1. robots.txt blocking — Check that your favicon file isn't blocked
  2. Wrong size — Ensure it's at least 48×48 and a multiple of 48
  3. Not square — Google rejects non-square images
  4. Low quality — Blurry, pixelated, or generic icons may be ignored
  5. Too new — Google takes days to weeks to process new favicons
  6. Different domain — Favicon hosted on a CDN with a different domain may not work

How to Check Your Favicon in Google

  1. Open Google Search Console
  2. Go to URL Inspection
  3. Enter your homepage URL
  4. Click "Request Indexing" to trigger a re-crawl
  5. Wait a few days and check search results for site:yourdomain.com

Favicon SEO Checklist

  • Provide a favicon that is 48×48 or larger (multiple of 48)
  • Use PNG or ICO format (not SVG — Google may not process it)
  • Declare it in your HTML with a <link rel="icon"> tag
  • Also place favicon.ico at the root as a fallback
  • Don't block it in robots.txt
  • Host it on the same domain as your website
  • Make it a square, branded image
  • Submit your site to Google Search Console and request indexing

Beyond Google: Favicons in Other Contexts

Favicons also appear in:

  • Bing search results — Similar to Google's implementation
  • Social media previews — Some platforms show favicons in link cards
  • Browser history and bookmarks — Helps users find your site again
  • Password managers — Show favicons next to saved credentials
  • Link aggregators — Sites like Reddit, Hacker News show favicons next to links

2019 年，Google 开始在移动端搜索结果中显示 Favicon。到 2023 年，桌面端也有了。如今，你的 Favicon 是 Google 每条搜索结果的可见组成部分。以下是为什么这很重要 — 以及如何做对。

Favicon 在 Google 搜索中的显示方式

在 Google 搜索结果中，你的 Favicon 出现在：

  • 移动端：在网站名称和 URL 旁边，标题链接上方
  • 桌面端：在标题下方的面包屑/URL 旁边

如果没有 Favicon（或不满足 Google 的要求），Google 会显示一个灰色的通用地球图标。这会让你的搜索结果看起来比有品牌 Favicon 的竞争对手更不可信。

对点击率的影响

点击率（CTR）是最重要的 SEO 指标之一。它衡量的是用户在搜索结果中看到你的列表后点击的频率。

Hostiserver 的一项研究发现，有 Favicon 的网站点击率提升高达 20%。为什么？

  • 视觉区分 — 你的列表在一片文字中脱颖而出
  • 品牌识别 — 回访用户在阅读标题前就能认出你的图标
  • 感知专业性 — 品牌图标表明这是一个合法的、维护中的网站
  • 信任信号 — 通用地球图标通常与垃圾网站或废弃网站相关联

Google 的技术要求

要求详情
尺寸48px 的倍数（如 48×48、96×96、144×144）。Google 显示时缩小到 16×16。
形状必须是正方形。非正方形图片可能被拒绝。
URL必须可抓取 — 不被 robots.txt 屏蔽。
稳定性URL 不应频繁更改。
内容必须代表网站品牌。不能有不当、冒犯性或通用内容。
域名必须从与网站相同的域名（或子域名）提供。

Google 不显示你的 Favicon 的常见原因

  1. robots.txt 屏蔽 — 检查你的 Favicon 文件没有被阻止
  2. 尺寸错误 — 确保至少 48×48 且是 48 的倍数
  3. 不是正方形 — Google 拒绝非正方形图片
  4. 质量太低 — 模糊、像素化或通用图标可能被忽略
  5. 太新 — Google 处理新 Favicon 需要几天到几周
  6. 不同域名 — 托管在不同域名 CDN 上的 Favicon 可能不行

如何在 Google 中检查你的 Favicon

  1. 打开 Google Search Console
  2. 进入 URL 检查
  3. 输入你的首页 URL
  4. 点击"请求编入索引"触发重新抓取
  5. 等几天后搜索 site:你的域名.com 检查结果

Favicon SEO 清单

  • 提供 48×48 或更大的 Favicon（48 的倍数）
  • 使用 PNG 或 ICO 格式（不是 SVG — Google 可能不处理它）
  • 在 HTML 中用 <link rel="icon"> 标签声明
  • 同时在根目录放一个 favicon.ico 作为后备
  • 不要在 robots.txt 中屏蔽它
  • 在与网站相同的域名上托管
  • 使其成为正方形的品牌图片
  • 将网站提交到 Google Search Console 并请求索引
