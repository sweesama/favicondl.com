The Simplest Favicon Downloader & Extractor

Mzu favicondl is a free online favicon downloader that lets you extract and download any website's favicon instantly. Simply enter a domain name or URL, choose your preferred icon size, and get the favicon — no sign-up, no installation, no limits.

Whether you're a web developer building a link preview, a designer collecting brand assets, or just curious about a site's icon, our favicon extractor handles it all. We support sizes from 16×16 to 512×512 pixels, and every download comes with a copy-ready HTML snippet and direct URL for easy integration into your project.

Favicons (short for "favorite icons") are the small icons displayed in browser tabs, bookmarks, and search results. They play a key role in brand recognition and user experience. Learn more about the favicon standard from the MDN Web Docs.
